Frequently Asked Questions

What is CloudSync Pro?

CloudSync Pro is a cloud-based file synchronization and backup service that automatically keeps your files in sync across all your devices, including desktops, laptops, and mobile devices.

How much storage do I get with a free account?

Free accounts include 5 GB of cloud storage. You can upgrade to our Plus plan for 100 GB or our Business plan for unlimited storage at any time.

Is my data encrypted?

Yes, all data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption both in transit and at rest. We also support optional client-side encryption where only you hold the decryption key.

Can I share files with people who do not have an account?

Yes, you can generate shareable links for any file or folder. Recipients can view and download shared files without needing a CloudSync Pro account. You can also set expiration dates and passwords on shared links.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Account Settings page under the Billing section. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period, and no further charges will be applied.
